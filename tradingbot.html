Learn how to build automated trading systems step by step.

About this page

This page is about learning on how to create automated trading using cTrader API. The goal is to help you understand the full journey—from connecting to cTrader, reading market data, designing strategies, and finally executing trades automatically.

What you will learn

  • Connecting to cTrader API: How to authenticate and access your trading account programmatically.
  • Market data: Fetching prices, candles, and order book information for your strategies.
  • Strategy design: Turning trading ideas into rules that a bot can execute.
  • Order execution: Placing, modifying, and closing trades via the API.
  • Risk management: Position sizing, stop losses, and protecting your capital.
